"GET STUCK IN THE MUD"

by POTTERS

Come and get your hands dirty and let your imagination loose shaping clay in all different forms and sizes. We offer all sorts of classes depending on your age, level and preferences.

Solo Sessions

Come and join us for a one-to-one session whith teachers who are experts in evry single level, you can choose from of our three ranges: Beginner-Intermediate-Advanced. These sessions are completly private and can be between 30-90 minutes long. Prices range from around 25€ an hour and need to be booked at least a week in advance to schedule the right day.

Group Clases

Here we have the perfect solution for a fun day out, a birthday present or a group of friends who have an interest in pottery! Come an join us for our group sessions that can be from 2 people up to 6. These ssions are 1 hour long and include wine, pottery lessons and you get to take home your masterpiece! Sessions are at a fixed price of 180€ so the more you are the better!

Online lessons

Finally here is our latest and newest way of teaching pottery that has never been done before... Learn from anywhere you want in the world! We are now introcing virtual sessions since the pandemic happened. These lessons are only 20€ an hour but requiere 3 things: A webcam, a Zoom account installed on any device and basic potter's utensiles: a potter's wheel, tools and clay.

ALl ages are available

Over the years we have had clients from all different places and of all ages. Potters is the perfect place to find a new talent and let your imagination fly. However we have a few simple rules you have to follow:

Number 1: All children must be accompanied by an adult

Number 2: We please ask our clients to be as punctual as possible as there is a high demand for our services.

Number 3: Always have fun and a smile on your face! It is important nit to get frustrated, pottery isn't about perfection it's about having fun and creating something original which shows your personality

Doing good through POTTERS

We are very conscious on giving back to society and helping out within our community. That is why 6 cents of every dollar we earn are donated to LOVE ORPHANAGE which is foundation that help all these local orphanages by providing entertainment, school materials, food and clothing. Also, every Tuesday night we invite children from different orphanages to distract them and show them new ways to enjoy activities outside of their residence.
